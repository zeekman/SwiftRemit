1. Login Screen
Focus: Speed and security.

Heading: Welcome Back

Sub-heading: Securely access your Swift Remit dashboard.

Field Labels:

Email or Username

Password

Primary Button: Login to Account →

Secondary Option: * Text: Or sign in faster with

Button: Biometric Login

Footer: New to Swift Remit? Create an account

2. Sign Up Screen
Focus: Trust and the value proposition of decentralization.

Heading: Join the Future of Remittance

Sub-heading: Create your decentralized account and start sending money globally in seconds.

Field Labels:

Email Address

Create Password

Confirm Password

Checkbox: I agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Primary Button: Create My Account

Footer: Already have an account? Log In

3. Forgot/Reset Password Screen
Focus: Security guidance and clarity.

Heading: Create New Password

Sub-heading: For your security, choose a strong password you haven't used with us before.

Password Checklist:

Minimum 8 characters

Include at least one number

Include a special character

Mix of upper & lowercase letters

Primary Button: Reset Password →

Success Indicator (Optional Tooltip): Password strength: Strong

These are Key Designs Made:
Action-Oriented Language: Changed "Login" to "Login to Account" and "Create Account" to "Create My Account" to make the interface feel more responsive to the user.

Security Reassurance: Reinforced the "Secured with End-to-End Encryption" badge by keeping the copy concise and high-contrast.

Consistency: Standardized the tone across all three screens to ensure the brand voice stays "Secure, Swift, and Simple."
